IBB - WDR

IBB-WDR is a highly efficient, real-time processing and able to dynamically process with local tone map engine at multi exposured levels enviroment and adjust the contrast ratios,adjusting each pixel of each frame dynamically to match the processing which occurs in the retina, and preserving image details.

*Features

- The preservation of the black and white points of the image (to prevent color clipping and avoid true blacks becoming gray)

- The preservation of true color

- The exact preservation of local contrast

- The complete elimination of any spatial artifacts, such as halos.
